Indulge in the classic! A touch of sweetness to start your day

Cuisine: French

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 15 mins

Total Time: 35 mins

This French Toast is a perfect weekend treat.

Ingredients

Method

  1. Preheat griddle to 350 degrees F .
  2. In a blender, add the eggs, milk, flour, sugar, salt, cinnamon, and vanilla. Blend until smooth
  3. Dip bread slices into the batter.
  4. dredging them well on both sides.
  5. and place on hot, greased griddle.
  6. Cook for a few minutes, until the bottom of the breads starts to get golden brown.
  7. Flip and cook on the other side the same.
  8. Remove to a plate.
  9. Finally, enjoy the French Toast Serve warm with syrup and a sprinkle of powdered sugar.
