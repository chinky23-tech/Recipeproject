Morning Boost or Evening Bite – Poha's Always Right!

Cuisine: Indian Food

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 10 mins

Total Time: 20 mins

Enjoy Poha – The Ultimate Anytime Comfort Food!

Ingredients

Method

  1. firstly, in a large bowl take 1.5 cup poha. do not take thin poha as it turns mushy as soon as you add water.
  2. rinse in water and drain off the water
  3. rest for 8-10 minutes, or until the poha softens without turning mushy or sticky.
  4. in a large kadai heat 2 tbsp oil and roast 2 tbsp peanut on low flame.
  5. roast until the peanuts turn crunchy, keep aside.
  6. in the same oil, add 1 tbsp mustard, and few curry leaves. splutter the tempering.
  7. now add 2 chilli, and 1 onion. saute until the onions softens slightly.
  8. further, add ¼ tbsp turmeric and ¼ tbsp salt, 1tbsp sugar. saute slightly.
  9. add soaked poha and mix well combining everything well.
  10. cover and simmer for 3 minutes or unitl the poha is cooked well.
  11. now add fried peanuts, 2 tbsp coriander and ½ lemon. mix well.
  12. finally, enjoy poha recipe with the mixture as a morning breakfast
