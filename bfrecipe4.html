Where Every Bite of Paneer Unfolds a Burst of Flavor!

Cuisine: Indian Food

Prep Time: 30 mins

Cook Time: 15 mins

Total Time: 45 mins

From Dawn to Dusk, Paneer Parantha Got You Covered

Ingredients

Method

  1. Paneer Stuffing: Firstly, in a large bowl take 2 cup paneer, ½ tsp cumin powder, 1 tsp aamchur, ½ tsp chilli powder, 1 inch ginger, grated, 2 chilli 2 onion, finely chopped, 2 tbsp coriander, and ½ tsp salt.
  2. Mix well making sure everything is well combined.
  3. paneer stuffing is ready, keep it aside.
  4. Dough: Firstly, in a large bowl take 2 cup of wheat flour ½ tsp salt and ½ tsp ajwain. mix well
  5. Knead to a smooth and soft dough adding water as required.Grease oil and rest for 20 minutes.
  6. Paneer Parantha:After the dough has rested for 20 minutes, knead slightly
  7. Now pinch a ball-sized dough and roll about 4 inches in diameter.Place a ball-sized prepared paneer stuffing in the centre.
  8. Take the edge and start pleating bringing it to the centre.Also, join the pleats together and secure tight pinching off excess dough.
  9. Sprinkle some wheat flour and roll slightly thick.
  10. On a hot tawa place the rolled paratha and cook for a minute
  11. Furthermore, flip the paratha when the base is partly cooked (after a minute).
  12. Also, brush oil/ghee and press slightly. flip again once or twice till both sides are cooked properly
  13. Finally, serve hot paneer paratha with sauce, raita or pickle.
