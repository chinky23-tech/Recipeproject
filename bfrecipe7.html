Quick & Easy Oats Chilla is perfect for busy mornings

Cuisine: Indian Food

Prep Time: 5 mins

Cook Time: 15 mins

Total Time: 20 mins

Packed with fiber and flavor - Nutritious Oats Chilla

Ingredients

Method

  1. Firstly dry roast 1 cup rolled oats. take unflavoured oats as we are adding spices.
  2. roast until the oats turn crisp.
  3. cool the oats completely and blend to a fine powder without adding any water.
  4. transfer the powdered oats into a large mixing bowl.
  5. also, add 2 tbsp rava for binding.
  6. now add ¼ cup curd and 1 cup water.whisk and mix make smooth lump free batter.
  7. additionally, add ¼ tsp turmeric, ½ tsp ginger paste, 2 chilli, ½ tsp crushed cumin.
  8. also add 2 tbsp onion, 2 tbsp tomato and ½ tsp salt.mix well forming thick flowing consistency chilla batter.
  9. further, pour a ladleful of batter onto a hot tawa and spread gently.pour ½ tsp of olive oil over chilla.
  10. cover and allow cooking on a medium flame for a minute.
  11. now flip the chilla and cook both sides pressing gently.
  12. finally, oats chilla is ready to serve with green chutney.
