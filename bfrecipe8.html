Crispy Aloo Parantha with a dollop of butter – pure comfort food.

Cuisine: Indian Food

Prep Time: 20 mins

Cook Time: 25 mins

Total Time: 45 mins

Spice up your morning with a delicious Aloo Parantha

Ingredients

Method

  1. First, boil or steam 4 medium-sized potatoes in a pressure cooker.
  2. Mash boil potatoes very well, with no lumps ,chunks or bits.
  3. ½ to 1 teaspoon finely chopped green chillies
  4. ¼ to ½ teaspoon punjabi garam masala powder
  5. ½ to 1 teaspoon dry mango powder,salt according to your taste
  6. mix the ground spices and green chilies into the mashed potatoes to get an even mixture.Set the prepared aloo stuffing aside.
  7. Further to prepare the dough, in a large bowl take 2 cup wheat flour, Add water as required and knead to smooth and soft dough. .
  8. Now pinch a ball-sized dough and roll about 4 inches in diameter.
  9. Place a ball-sized prepared allu stuffing in the centre.Take the edge and start pleating bringing it to the centre.
  10. Also, join the pleats together and secure tight pinching off excess dough.Sprinkle some wheat flour and roll slightly thick.
  11. On a hot tawa place the rolled paratha and cook for a minute.
  12. Furthermore, flip the paratha when the base is partly cooked (after a minute).
  13. Also, brush oil/ghee and press slightly. flip again once or twice till both sides are cooked properly.
  14. Finally, serve hot Allu paratha with sauce, raita or pickle
