Packed with nutrients and bursting with flavor - Spinach omelette

Cuisine: Indian Food

Prep Time: 4 mins

Cook Time: 5 mins

Total Time: 9 mins

Green goodness in every bite!

Ingredients

Method

  1. Firstly crack the eggs into a bowl and a tablespoon of milk.
  2. Thinly slice the garlic. Add a pinch of salt.
  3. Wash and chop the spinach leaves.
  4. And whisk the eggs gently for the yolk to mix well.
  5. Heat a frying pan.
  6. And add the chopped spinach , garlic and sprinkle some salt.
  7. Let the leaves wilt, and take them off the pan.
  8. Pour a tsp oil on the pan and spread it evenly with a spatula .
  9. Reduce the flame to medium, and pour the egg mixture.
  10. Tilt the pan and let the eggs spread evenly.
  11. As soon as the omelette start to set , lift near the edges with the spatula so that the uncooked eggs can reach the bottom of the pan.
  12. Once the center starts to set, Now sprinkle the grated cheese on top of it.
  13. Lower the flame and cook further . wait for the cheese to start melting.and once the omelette starts to get detached from sides.
  14. fold in half. Gently slide the omelette to a plate.Enjoy Spinach omelette as a healthy morning breakfast.
