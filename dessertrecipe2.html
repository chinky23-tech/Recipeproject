Nothing beats the taste of homemade Falooda

Cuisine: North India

Prep Time: 30 mins

Cook Time: 15 mins

Total Time: 45 mins

Flavors Folded to Perfection – Falooda Delight.

Ingredients

Method

  1. Soak basil seeds in water for about 20 to 30 mins. Once they swell using a strainer drain the extra water and set the soaked seeds aside.
  2. Prepare the falooda sev
  3. soaking the falooda sev in warm water. Or you can cook them in hot water until softened.
  4. Once cooked, using strainer or colander, drain the extra water. Then rinse the cooked falooda sev in fresh water. Drain the water again. Keep covered aside till they are cooled completely at room temperature.
  5. Chop the nuts and set aside.
  6. In 4 tall glasses each, add 1.5 to 2 tablespoons rose syrup. Depending upon the sweetness preferred and the size of the glass add less or more of the rose syrup.
  7. Then add 2 tablespoons soaked basil seeds.
  8. Add 2 to 3 tablespoons of the faluda sev.
  9. In a slow stream, pour about 1 cup milk.
  10. Then top with one scoop of vanilla ice cream.
  11. Garnish with a few raisins, sliced pistachios, almonds or cashews. For a bright pop of color, you can decorate with a few glazed cherries or tutti-frutti.
  12. Serve the falooda straight away. While drinking, with a spoon mix the rose syrup settled at the bottom with the milk and then drink as you eat the flooda sev , basil seeds and nuts.
