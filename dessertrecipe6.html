Nothing beats the taste of homemade kheer

Cuisine: North India

Prep Time: 20 mins

Cook Time: 30 mins

Total Time: 50 mins

Flavors Folded to Perfection – Kheer Delight.

Ingredients

Method

  1. Rinse ¼ cup basmati rice a couple of times till the water runs clear of the starch.
  2. Then soak the rice in enough water for 15 to 20 mins.
  3. Meanwhile, when the rice grains are soaking, take whole or full fat milk in a heavy wide pan.
  4. Keep the pan on a low to medium-low heat. Stir at intervals so that the milk does not stick at the bottom of the pan.
  5. Let the milk come to a boil.
  6. Take 1 tablespoon milk from the pan in a small bowl. Let the milk become warm. Then add a few saffron strands in the milk. Keep aside.
  7. After the milk begins to boil, drain all the water from the rice and add it to the hot boiling milk. mix very well.
  8. Simmer and cook rice on a low heat. No need to cover the pan when the rice is cooking.
  9. Cook the rice grains till they are 50% done or half-cooked.
  10. Then add sugar. You can add more or less sugar according to your preferences. Mix the sugar in the milk with a spoon.
  11. Continue to cook rice on a low to medium-low heat. Do stir at intervals. Simmer till the rice is almost cooked.
  12. Then add ½ tsp green cardamom powder.sliced almonds and chopped cashews.and sliced pistachios & golden raisins. Mix well.Then add the saffron infused milk.
  13. Switch off the heat when the rice grains are completely cooked. The kheer will also thicken.
  14. You can enjoy it cold or warm it before serving.
