Nothing beats the taste of homemade Mango Lassi

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 15 mins

Total Time: 25 mins

Method

  1. Add mango, milk, yogurt, mango pulp (start with lower amount), cane sugar (start with the lower amount), and ice cubes to a high-speed blender.
  2. Blend on high until smooth and no longer icy. If the mixture gets stuck, use the blender tamper or stop and push it down with a spatula.
  3. Taste and add remaining sugar for sweetness, pulp for more mango.
  4. flavor/color, or 1-2 tbsp of yogurt for tartness.
