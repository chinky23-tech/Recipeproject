Enjoy Kadhi Pakora – The Ultimate Anytime Comfort Food!

Cuisine: North India

Prep Time: 20 mins

Cook Time: 60 mins

Total Time: 80 mins

A classic comfort food - Kadhi Pakora

Ingredients

Method

  1. In a bowl take 1.5 cups sour curd . Whisk it well till smooth.
  2. Add 8 tablespoons of gram flour (besan), ½ teaspoon red chilli powder, ½ teaspoon turmeric powder, ½ teaspoon garam masala powder and 1 teaspoon salt to the whisked curd.
  3. Stir and mix everything again.
  4. Add 3 cups water and stir again.
  5. Stir very well to make a smooth mixture without lumps.
  6. Firstly take a kadai , heat 2 tbsp of mustard oil
  7. Add 1 teaspoon cumin seeds, 8 to 10 fenugreek seeds (methi seeds) and a generous pinch of asafoetida (hing).
  8. Allow the cumin seeds to crackle and the fenugreek seeds to change their color. Fry on a low flame, so that these spices do not get burnt.
  9. Add ⅓ cup chopped onions. Stir and saute for 3 minutes on low to medium-low heat.
  10. Then add 1 tablespoon chopped ginger, ¾ to 1 tablespoon chopped garlic and 2 green chillies, chopped. Stir and sauté for a minute.
  11. Now add 8 to 10 curry leaves, 2 dry red chilies (halved or broken).
  12. Stir and sauté for a minute on a low heat.
  13. Then add the curd slurry.Stir very well.
  14. Increase the flame to medium and bring the kadhi to a boil. Keep on stirring often so that the bottom does not get browned. Time taken to cook kadhi will be around 14 to 16 minutes.
  15. After it comes to a boil, then lower the heat and simmer it for a further 6 to 7 minutes. Do stir at times
  16. The kadhi will thicken as it cooks and simmers. If it becomes too thick, then add some hot water.let it cook on simmer for some time.
  17. Now add the onion pakora to the kadhi. Stir gently.
  18. Cover with a lid and let the onion pakora be soaked in it for 8 to 10 minutes.
  19. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve Punjabi Kadhi Pakora hot with steamed rice or jeera rice, topped with few teaspoonfuls of ghee.
