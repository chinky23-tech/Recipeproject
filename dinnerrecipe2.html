Enjoy Butter Chicken – The Ultimate Anytime Comfort Food!

Cuisine: North India

Prep Time: 20 mins

Cook Time: 60 mins

Total Time: 80 mins

A classic comfort food - Butter Chicken

Ingredients

Method

  1. Heat the ghee (butter or oil) over high heat in a large fry pan.
  2. Take the chicken out of the Marinade but do not wipe or shake off the marinade from the chicken (but don’t pour the Marinade left in the bowl into the fry pan).
  3. Place chicken in the fry pan and cook for around 3 minutes, or until the chicken is white all over (it doesn’t really brown because of the Marinade).
  4. Sauce: Add the tomato puree, cream, sugar and salt. Also add any remaining marinade left in the bowl.
  5. Turn down to low and simmer for 20 minutes. Do a taste test to see if it needs more salt
  6. Garnish with coriander leaves. Serve with basmati rice.
