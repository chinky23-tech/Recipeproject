Enjoy Channa masala – The Ultimate Anytime Comfort Food!

Cuisine: North India

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 20 mins

Total Time: 40 mins

A classic comfort food - Channa Masala

Ingredients

Method

  1. Rinse and then soak 1 cup of white chickpeas/chana with enough water covering them overnight or for 8 to 9 hours. In the morning discard the water.
  2. Then add the soaked chickpeas with 3 cups of water in a 3 litre stovetop pressure cooker.
  3. Pressure cook the chickpeas for 12 to 14 whistles or about 14 to 16 minutes or until they are tender and cooked completely.
  4. The chickpeas should be cooked well and must be soft when you mash it between your fingers or a spoon.
  5. Once the chana are cooked then drain the water and place boiled chickpeas aside.
  6. Heat oil in a pan and add the sliced onions.
  7. Sauté the onions until they become translucent and are softened.
  8. Add 1.5 teaspoons of ginger-garlic paste and a pinch of asofoetida .
  9. Also add 1 to 2 slit green chilies.
  10. Sauté on low heat for a minute, until the raw aroma from the ginger – garlic paste dissipates. Even the color of the onions would change to pale light brown.
  11. Sprinkle the black pepper powder and salt as required. Stir and mix well.
  12. Now add the boiled chana. Stir well to combine.
  13. Add the lime or lemon juice and mix well.
  14. Cover with a lid and simmer chana curry for 2 to 3 mins on low heat. Remove the lid and turn off the heat.
  15. Garnish the Dry Chana Masala with 1 tablespoon of chopped coriander leaves and 8 to 10 mint leaves.
  16. Serve hot or warm. Drizzle some lime juice on the chana while eating.
