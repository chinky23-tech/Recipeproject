Cuisine: North India

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 20 mins

Total Time: 30 mins

A classic comfort food - Mix Veg

Ingredients

Method

  1. Heat 1tbsp oil in a large pan or kadahi over medium heat.
  2. Add green chilies and ginger and cook for about a minute.
  3. Add chopped onions fry for 2-3 minutes and add chopped tomatoes.
  4. Cook all of these till tomatoes are pulpy.Switch off the heat and allow it to cool.After it cools down, blend it to a smooth paste. Keep it aside.
  5. Now heat another tablespoon of peanut oil. Add cumin seeds, when cumin starts to pop up reduce the heat to minimum. and add chopped mix vegetables one by one.
  6. Cook everything till 50% cooked
  7. Saute them for a minute and add 3-4 tbsp of water with ground masala paste of onion and tomatoes.
  8. Now add salt, Kashmiri red chilli powder, garam masala powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, and salt.
  9. Cook for 10 minutes while stirring occasionally.
  10. After 10 minutes check if vegetables are cooked through add cream mix well and serve in a serving dish.
  11. Sprinkle some chopped fresh coriander leaves and serve mix veg sabzi with fresh paratha or Roti.
