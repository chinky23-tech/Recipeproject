Blueberry , Cranberry & Mint Crush

Cuisine: North India

Prep Time: 5 mins

Total Time: 5 mins

Blueberries, cranberry and lime blitzed together to a refreshing summer mocktail that'll make you feel good.

Ingredients

Method

  1. Place lime juice, cranberry juice, frozen blueberries and 10 mint leaves in a blender. Blend until smooth
  2. Add ice. Blend until well combined. Pour into 2 tall glasses, add lime slices and extra mint leaves and serve
