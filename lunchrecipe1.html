The King of Beans, Spice up your day with Rajma.

Cuisine: North India

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 40 mins

Total Time: 50 mins

A classic comfort food - Rajma

Ingredients

Method

  1. Add rajma to a large bowl and rinse them thrice . Soak them for 8 hours.
  2. Transfer them to a stovetop pressure cooker. pour water as required.pressure cook until soft
  3. Heat ghee or oil in a pot. add cumin seeds and bay leaf. when they begin to sizzle add the onions and green chilli.
  4. Saute on a medium heat until golden.stir in the ginger garlic and saute for a minute until aromatic.
  5. Pour the tomato puree & cook until the onion tomato mixture turns thick, for about 3 min.
  6. stir in the spices - red chilli powder, garam masala, coriander powder, cumin powder and turmeric.saute for 2 to 3 minutes , until the masala begins to aromatic
  7. Add the rajma along with the stock.
  8. Mix well and smimmer for 10 to 15 mins, until slightly thick.
  9. Taste test and add spices if required . Add crushed kasuri methi
  10. Stir in cream and garnish with coriander leaves and ginger juliennes.
  11. Serve warm rajma with rice.
