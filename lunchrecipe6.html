Enjoy Idli sambhar – The Ultimate Anytime Comfort Food!

Cuisine: India

Prep Time: 30 mins

Cook Time: 45 mins

Total Time: 75 mins

A classic comfort food - Idli Sambhar

Ingredients

Method

  1. Idli :firstly, in a large pan add in rava, 1 cup curd, ½ tsp salt and combine well without forming any lumps
  2. allow to rest for 20 minutes or till rava / sooji absorbs water.
  3. again add ¼ cup water or as required to prepare idli batter consistency
  4. just before steaming the idlis, add in ¼ tsp eno fruit salt or pinch of baking soda and give a gentle mix.
  5. pour the batter immediately onto greased idli plate.
  6. steam them on medium flame for 15 minutes or till the idli are cooked completely.
  7. Sambhar : firstly, in a large kadai heat, 2 tsp oil and splutter tempering.
  8. add 2 chilli and saute for a minute till it changes colour.further, add 1 tomato and continue to cook.
  9. now add mixed vegetables and saute for 2 minutes.further, add ¼ tsp turmeric, ½ tsp jaggery, 1 tsp salt and 1 cup water.
  10. mix well, cover and boil for 10 minutes.now add ¾ cup tamarind extract and mix well.
  11. cover and boil for 10 minutes. additionally, add 1 cup toor dal, water and mix well
  12. add in 4 tsp of prepared sambar powder. boil for a minute, until the flavours are well absorbed. finally, enjoy idli sambar with hot steamed idli.
