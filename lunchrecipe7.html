Enjoy Dal Tadka – The Ultimate Anytime Comfort Food!

Cuisine: North India

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 15 mins

Total Time: 25 mins

A classic comfort food - Dal Tadka

Ingredients

Method

  1. firstly, in a pressure cooker take 1 cup toor dal and ½ cup moong dal. make sure to rinse the dal well.
  2. add 2 tomato, 1 onion, 10 cloves garlic, 1 bay leaf, 1 black cardamom, ½ tsp turmeric, and 1 tsp salt.
  3. also, add 2 tsp ghee and 4 cup water.
  4. cover and pressure cook for 5 whistles or until the dal is cooked well.
  5. mash the dal slightly and adjust consistency as required.
  6. to prepare the tempering, heat 2 tbsp ghee.
  7. add 4 cloves garlic and fry for a minute.
  8. also add 1 tsp cumin, 2 tsp kasuri methi, 1 tsp chilli powder, pinch hing.
  9. splutter the tempering and pour over the dal.
  10. add in 2 tbsp coriander and mix well.
  11. finally, enjoy cooker dal tadka with jeera rice.
