Enjoy chicken curry – The Ultimate Anytime Comfort Food!

Cuisine: North India

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 15 mins

Total Time: 25 mins

A classic comfort food - chicken curry

Ingredients

Method

  1. On a medium flame, heat oil in a deep pan. Add the whole spices - bay leaf.cinnamon, cloves & green cardamoms.
  2. Stir in the chopped onions and chillies .Saute until deep golden in color, for 7 to 8 mins.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste and saute till you get a nice aroma, for about a minute.
  4. Then add tomatoes, turmeric and salt.Mix well & cook until the tomatoes turn soft & mushy.
  5. Turn down the heat to low ,stir in the yogurt , red chilli powder,garam masala & coriander powder.
  6. Cook on low heat, until the mixture smells aromatic.
  7. Add chicken and coriander leaves . fry on a medium heat till the chicken turns white or pale in color. this takes around 3 mins.
  8. Cover and cook on a low heat for 3 to 4 minutes. This step is very important as the chicken absorbs all the flavours of the spices.
  9. Meanwhile in a separate pot, heat up 1 cup water.
  10. For chicken curry: Pour hot water just enough to make a thick gravy.
  11. Cover and cook on a medium flame till the chicken is soft cooked and the curry turns thick.
  12. Lastly garnish with coriander leaves . Finally , serve chicken curry with butter naan/ jeera rice.
