Tantalize Your Taste Buds with Pasta , a bite of flavor

Cuisine: North India

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 90 mins

Total Time: 105 mins

Pasta Delights to Satisfy Cravings.

Ingredients

Method

  1. Put a large pot of salted water on the stovetop on high heat.
  2. In a large skillet heat 2 teaspoons of olive oil on medium-high heat. Add the ground beef and cook until it is lightly browned on all sides. Remove the beef with a slotted spoon to a bowl. Drain off all but a tablespoon of fat.
  3. Add the diced bell pepper and onions to the skillet . Cook for 4 to 5 minutes, until the onions are translucent and the peppers softened. Add the minced garlic and cook half a minute more.
  4. Return the browned ground beef to the pan. Stir to combine, reduce the heat to low, and cook for another 5 minutes.
  5. Make the sauce: Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste to the pot.Add the parsley, oregano, and Italian seasonings, adjusting the amounts to taste. Sprinkle with garlic powder and/or garlic salt, to taste.
  6. Sprinkle with red or white vinegar. Stir in sugar, a tablespoon at a time,
  8. Add salt to taste, and note that you will later be adding Parmesan, which is salty.Cook for 15 to 45 minutes, stirring often. Scrape the bottom of the pot every so often so nothing sticks to the bottom and scorches.
  9. Boil and drain the lasagna noodles:Spread a little olive oil on a large rimmed baking sheet, and lay out the cooked noodles on this sheet, turning them over so that they get coated with a little of the olive oil. This will prevent them from sticking together.
  10. Preheat the oven to 375°F.Assemble the lasagna:
  11. In a 9x13-inch lasagna dish, ladle a cup of sauce and spread it over the bottom of the dish. Arrange one layer of lasagna noodles lengthwise, over the sauce. Ladle a third of the remaining sauce over the noodles.
  12. Sprinkle a layer of a third of the grated mozzarella on top of the lasagna sauce. Add half of the ricotta cheese, by placing cheese dollops every couple of inches. Sprinkle half the grated parmesan cheese evenly over the top of the ricotta cheese.
  13. Apply the second layer of noodles and top it with half of the remaining sauce. Add half of the remaining mozzarella, the remaining ricotta cheese, and another the remaining Parmesan.
  14. Finish with another layer of noodles. Spread the remaining sauce over the top layer of noodles and sprinkle with the remaining mozzarella cheese.
  15. Bake: Cover the lasagna pan with aluminum foil, tented slightly so it doesn't touch the noodles or sauce). Bake at 375°F for 45 minutes. Uncover in the last 10 minutes if you'd like more of a crusty top or edges.
  16. Allow the lasagna to cool for at least 15 before serving.
