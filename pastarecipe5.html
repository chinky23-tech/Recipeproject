Tantalize Your Taste Buds with Pasta , a bite of flavor

Cuisine: Italian

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 15 mins

Total Time: 30 mins

Pasta Delights to Satisfy Cravings.

Ingredients

Method

  1. Heat a pan and add lightly toast almonds for 2 to 3 minutes on a low heat. Set aside in a tray or plate for the almonds to cool.
  2. Rinse the basil leaves using a strainer or colander.
  3. Spread them on a tray or a dry colander or strainer so that they dry naturally. You can use a salad spinner to dry the leaves.
  4. Making Basil Almond Pesto
  5. Once the almonds cool at room temperature, add them in a food processor or food chopper or a small to medium grinder jar.Process and crush them coarsely.
  6. Add the basil leaves which has been rinsed first and then dried naturally. Also add the garlic cloves. Optionally you can also whole black peppers. Season with salt.crush the leaves.
  7. Grind to a semi fine or chunky consistency according to your preferences. You can also add a few drops of lemon juice to preserve the green color.
  8. Remove the pesto in a mixing bowl or pan. Use a spatula to remove the pesto from the sides and bottom of the food processor, food chopper or grinder jar. Cover and set aside.
  9. Bring water and salt to a boil on a medium to high heat.then add the pasta.Cook the pasta till they are al dente.With a slotted spoon take the pasta from the pan. Drain the water very well.
  10. Add the cooked pasta in a mixing bowl. Add all of the prepared pesto sauce.
  11. Mix very well. If you want you can add some lemon juice or crushed black pepper or a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil.
  12. Serve Pesto Pasta garnished with some parmesan cheese.
