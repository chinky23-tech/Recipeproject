Tantalize Your Taste Buds with Pasta , a bite of flavor

Cuisine: Italian

Prep Time: 20 mins

Cook Time: 30 mins

Total Time: 50 mins

Pasta Delights to Satisfy Cravings.

Ingredients

Method

  1. Cook the elbow pasta as per the instructions on the package till they are al dente. I first heated the water and salt to a vigorous boil and then added the pasta.
  2. Cook them till they are al dente or just cooked. They should not get too soft or mushy or break.
  3. Drain them in a colander or strainer.
  4. Now grease a baking pan with some softened butter. Skip this step if you do not plan to bake the mac n cheese.
  5. In a microwave safe bowl, melt butter or you can melt the butter in a small pan. Then add the breadcrumbs.
  6. With a spoon mix the breadrcumbs with the melted butter very well. Keep aside.
  7. Heat butter in a thick bottomed sauce pan on a low flame. Also preheat the oven to 200 degrees Celsius.
  8. The butter should heat up well. Then add the all purpose flour.
  9. Using a wired whisk stir the flour quickly as soon as you add it.Keep on stirring so that no lumps are formed.
  10. The frequent stirring helps the flour to cook evenly.Saute the flour till you get a nice cooked aroma from the flour and a pale golden color. Don’t brown the flour.
  11. Keep the flame to the lowest and then pour the milk in a gentle stream. Pour the milk with one hand and stir the sauce mixture with the other. The milk has to be chilled, so that lumps are not formed. If you use hot or warm milk or milk at room temperature, lumps can be formed.
  12. Do stir frequently while the milk warms and heats up. The sauce would begin to thicken, so do stir often.
  13. When the sauce has thickened well and coats the back of a spoon, switch off the flame.Keep the saucepan down and wait for a minute. Then add the shredded cheese.
  14. Again mix and stir so that the cheese melts.Now add the pasta in the cheese sauce.With a spoon stir and mix very well.The mac and cheese is done.
