Tantalize Your Taste Buds with Peanut Masala , A Bite of Flavour

Cuisine: North India

Prep Time: 5 mins

Total Time: 5 mins

Peanut Masala to Satisfy Cravings.

Ingredients

Method

  1. Finely chop the onion, cilantro, and chili pepper. Seed and chop the tomatoes, . I recommend you try the dish with half a chopped chili pepper first and add the other half later if needed.
  2. Toss the chopped vegetables in a bowl with the peanuts.
  3. Pour over the lemon juice and mix.
  4. Sprinkle with the chaat masala and mix again.
  5. Serve immediately.
