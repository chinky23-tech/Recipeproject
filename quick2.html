Tantalize Your Taste Buds with Kurkure Bhel , A Bite of Flavour

Cuisine: North India

Prep Time: 5mins

Total Time: 5 mins

Kurkure Bhel to Satisfy Cravings.

Ingredients

Method

  1. In a deep serving plate or bowl, add in Kurkure chips.
  2. Add chopped onion, tomato and coriander.
  3. Follow by adding chaat masala, green chutney, salt and pepper.
  4. Toss it all together until evenly mixed.
  5. Drizzle up the Tamarind Chutney and sprinkle with Aloo Bhujia.
  6. Serve and Enjoy!
