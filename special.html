Avocado toast is creamy, crisp and so satisfying, simple breakfast

Cuisine: Australia

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 5 mins

Total Time: 15 mins

Fuel your day with this light and crispy Avacado Toast.

Ingredients

Method

  1. Preheat griddle to 350 degrees F .
  2. Toast your slice of bread until golden and firm.
  3. Remove the pit from your avocado.
  4. Use a big spoon to scoop out the flesh. .
  5. Put it in a bowl and mash it up with a fork until it’s as smooth as you like it.
  6. Mix in a pinch of salt (about ⅛ teaspoon).
  7. and add more to taste, if desired.Spread avocado on top of your toast.
  8. Remove to a plate.
  9. Finally, enjoy the Avacado Toast perfect breakfast.
